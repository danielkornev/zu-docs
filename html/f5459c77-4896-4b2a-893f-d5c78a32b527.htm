Content is passed to and from Zet Universe as Entities. From a developer’s perspective, Entities are metadata elements, characterized by the Kind and an optional URI as a link to an external resource. From a user’s perspective, Entities (characterized by their Kinds) are everyday things that have semantically appropriate behavior, appearance, and actions.
